Science Topics

Impulse

It is the product of the net force acting on an object and the time interval during which the force is applied. Formula: J=ft (force x time)

Momentum

It is the product of the mass of an object and its velocity. Formula: p=mv (mass x velocity)

Valence Electrons

They are found on the outermost shell of an atom and they have the highest energy level.
